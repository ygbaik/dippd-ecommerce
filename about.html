Dear Dippers

Dipp’d is for distinct individuals who instinctively search for eccentric product offerings that match their blunt and bold personality.

I recognize that everyone has their own story and their own upbringing that made them who they are today. I want to celebrate the diveristy of our community, not just in race but in personality. My vision for this shop was to bring more awareness in the uniqueness of everyone who resides in Toronto.

At Dipp’d, we hope to create a community of doughnut lovers who are free to express themselves and enjoy a sweet treat that matches their pride of their own individuality. As the founder of this donut shop, I really hope you feel at home with one of our doughnuts in your hands.

Go get Dipp’d with your next order — we hope you enjoy!

-David Baiker, Donut Dipper
